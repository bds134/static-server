Featured Sites (Marked-up Text)

Welcome to New York City! This travel guide will take you to some of the city's most famous sites: Empire State Building, a towering landmark in Midtown Manhattan.
Next, visit the historic Statue of Liberty on Liberty Island, a symbol of freedom.
For a glimpse of New York's bustling transit, stop by Grand Central Terminal.
Enjoy nature at Central Park, the city's iconic green space.
Art lovers should explore the Museum of Modern Art and the grand Metropolitan Museum of Art.
Don't miss Columbus Circle, a major landmark and gateway to Central Park.
Experience the energy of Times Square, famous for its lights and crowds.
Finally, relax at Washington Square Park, a lively spot in Greenwich Village.
